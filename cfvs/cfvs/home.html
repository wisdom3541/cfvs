About Our School

A Place of Growth, Joy & Discovery

Our Programmes

Learning Tailored for Every Stage

We offer structured programmes designed to match your child's developmental journey from their very first school day.

🐣
Nursery
Ages 2 – 4

A gentle introduction to school life through play-based learning, building social skills and early language development.

  • Play-based curriculum
  • Emotional & social development
  • Phonics & early numeracy
  • Creative arts & sensory play
🌱
Kindergarten
Ages 4 – 6

Building confidence and curiosity through structured activities that bridge play and formal learning beautifully.

  • Reading & writing readiness
  • Mathematics foundations
  • Science discovery
  • Team activities & sports
📚
Primary School
Ages 6 – 12

A rigorous yet joyful academic programme that prepares pupils for secondary education and lifelong achievement.

  • Core academic subjects
  • ICT & digital literacy
  • Leadership & values education
  • Extracurricular clubs
